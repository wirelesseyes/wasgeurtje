About wasgeurtje

Laundry fragrance is a luxury laundry perfume for the laundry, our products come directly from Italy. Laundry fragrance is made for consumers who are looking for a new, child-friendly and environmentally conscious fragrance sensation for the laundry. Our fresh and delicate laundry fragrances are paraben free (E numbers) and made on the basis of essential oils. Our delicious scents are mixed with the laundry at the end of your wash cycle. Because they are essential oils, our scents linger for an extra long time. This also makes the environment in which you clean up the laundry fragrant. Laundry scent ensures that you start and end every washing day with a happy smile.
